```

├── "What Is [Topic]?" explanation (200-300 words)

├── Cost Table #2: City-by-city comparison

├── Process Steps (5-7 numbered steps)
├── Permit/Legal section
├── Cost Comparison table (Method A vs B)
├── FAQ section (5-8 questions, schema markup)
├── CTA: Free estimate / Price Check
└── Related articles links
```

## 5. Content Production Guidelines

### Every Blog Article Must Include:

1. **H1**: [Primary Keyword] + [City/Province] + [Year]
2. **Quick Summary Box** at top (3-5 key stats)
3. **Table of Contents** (auto-generated or manual)
4. **At least 2 data tables** (cost comparisons, method comparisons)
5. **FAQ Section** (5-8 questions) with `@type: FAQPage` JSON-LD
6. **Breadcrumb JSON-LD** (`@type: BreadcrumbList`)
7. **Article JSON-LD** (`@type: Article` with datePublished, dateModified, author)
8. **Internal links** to 3-5 relevant /costs, /services, /savings pages
9. **CTA** to /price-check or /pros (or both)
10. **Meta description** under 155 characters with primary keyword
11. **2,000-3,500 words** (sweet spot for ranking)
12. **Images** with descriptive alt text

### Content Tone
- Authoritative but approachable (not salesy)
- Ontario-specific data and regulations (OBC references)
- "We protect your money" angle — tie everything back to RenoNext's escrow/GPS/trust model
- Include real numbers — avoid vague statements like "costs vary"

### Internal Linking Strategy
```
Blog Article
├── Links TO /costs/[service] (detailed pricing)
├── Links TO /services/[slug] (full service overview)
├── Links TO /savings/[city] (rebates & incentives)
├── Links TO /price-check (conversion CTA)
├── Links TO /pros (browse contractors)
└── Links TO other blog articles (topic clusters)

/costs pages ← already link to /services
/services pages ← already link to /costs
/savings pages ← already link to /costs
```
